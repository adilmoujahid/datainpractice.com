AM207: Monte Carlo methods, stochastic optimization
// tags  Python MCMC

Monte Carlo methods are a diverse class of algorithms that rely on repeated random sampling to compute the solution to problems whose solution space is too large to explore systematically or whose systemic behavior is too complex to model. This Harvard University course introduces important principles of Monte Carlo techniques and demonstrates the power of these techniques with simple applications.
